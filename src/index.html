The Cage List

"Nobody wants to watch perfection."

Every week, my friend and I have a standing movie night that’s become a ritual we both look forward to. We pick a Nicolas Cage film, grab a beer, and dive into his unpredictable, often wild world of acting. It’s the perfect way to unwind and share a few laughs, experiencing everything Cage brings to the screen—from the surprisingly brilliant to the utterly bizarre.

Some weeks, we’ll stumble on a genuinely good movie, while other times, we’re left scratching our heads at the over-the-top plots or outrageous acting choices. Either way, a cold beer is essential—some films are definitely better with a bit of liquid encouragement, especially the more "unique" ones.

This list has become a sort of log, a growing record of every Nicolas Cage movie we’ve tackled together, capturing our reactions and favorite moments from each wild viewing.
